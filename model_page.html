Some Notes on Use

The placement of the Applied Force is controlled by the "Offset" slider.

The Torque displayed is the torque to be applied by the current force configuration for 1s when "Kick" is clicked. The Angular Momentum is the current angular momentum of the object.

Camera movement is enabled under "camera controls"
